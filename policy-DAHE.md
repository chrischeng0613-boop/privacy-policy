Last Updated: April 2026

We do NOT collect or store any personal data through Facebook Login, as our apps do not support this feature. However, we may use Meta (Facebook) SDKs and other third-party tools to collect non-personal information such as device identifiers (AAID), app usage statistics, and ad performance data.

The data collected is used solely for optimizing advertisement performance, analyzing app stability, and improving user experience.

3. Data Deletion & Contact

Our apps may contain links to the Google Play Store or other third-party services.
