Privacy Policy for OpenClaw

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Data Collection

OpenClaw is a private advertising management tool. We only access your Facebook Ad Account data (ID, Name, Status, and Spend) through official Facebook OAuth2 protocols.

2. Use of Data

The collected data is used exclusively for:

3. Data Storage & Privacy

All data is stored on a self-hosted private server. We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to any third parties or external services.

Users can revoke access at any time via Facebook App Settings. To request immediate deletion of all locally stored data, please contact us at: chrischeng0613@gmail.com.
